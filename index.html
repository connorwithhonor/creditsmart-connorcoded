Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How long does the credit repair process take? +

Most clients see measurable improvement within 60 to 90 days. The exact timeline depends on the number and type of negative items on your report. Some items can be resolved in as little as 30 days, while more complex cases may take 4 to 6 months for full resolution.

Is credit repair legal? +

Absolutely. Credit repair is 100% legal and is protected under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), which gives every consumer the right to dispute inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable information on their credit reports. Credit Smart Repair operates in full compliance with the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA) and all applicable federal and state laws.

What types of items can you remove? +

We challenge collections, charge-offs, late payments, bankruptcies, judgments, liens, repossessions, foreclosures, and any item that is inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable. If an item cannot be verified by the credit bureau within 30 days, the law requires its removal.

Do you work with clients outside of California? +

Yes. Credit Smart Repair serves clients in all 50 states. Our process is entirely digital, so geographic location is never a barrier. Whether you're in Los Angeles or New York, you receive the same expert service.

How is Credit Smart different from other credit repair companies? +

Most credit repair companies use generic template disputes. Credit Smart Repair takes a personalized, strategic approach. Robert Montoya personally oversees every case. You work directly with a credit expert, not a call center. And our results speak for themselves: consistent 100 to 150+ point increases for our clients.

I'm a loan officer. How does the partner program work? +

Simple. When a client doesn't qualify due to credit issues, you refer them to Credit Smart Repair. We restore their credit, and they come back to you mortgage-ready. You keep the relationship and close the deal. We also offer a formal affiliate partnership with recurring revenue opportunities. Contact us for details.
